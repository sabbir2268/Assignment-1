Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Embracing technology to drive innovation across various sectors including education, healthcare, and commerce.

Sustainability

Bangladesh is focusing on sustainable environmental practices to combat climate change and protect its natural resources.

Infrastructure

Development of smart cities and urban infrastructure to accommodate the growing urban population and enhance quality of life.

Empowerment

Promoting gender equality and women’s empowerment through various social and economic initiatives.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

The Digital Transformation of Bangladesh, anchored in the “Digital Bangladesh” vision, is a multifaceted journey of embracing technology.

Green Energy

Green Energy in Bangladesh refers to the transition towards renewable and sustainable energy sources such as solar, wind, biogas, and hydro power.

Modern Infrastructure

Modern infrastructure development in Bangladesh has been a catalyst for economic growth and social progress.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus is a Bangladeshi economist and founder of the Grameen Bank, renowned for pioneering the concepts of microcredit and microfinance. Awarded the Nobel Peace Prize in 2006, Yunus revolutionized the fight against poverty by providing small loans to the impoverished, especially women, fostering economic empowerment and self-sufficiency.His innovative banking model has been replicated globally, solidifying his influence as a leader in sustainable development and financial inclusion.
